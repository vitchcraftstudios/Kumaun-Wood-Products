Manufacturing Process

From agricultural waste to premium tableware, our process is 100% circular and eco-friendly, located in Sitarganj, Uttarakhand.

🎋 1. Sugarcane: Sourcing raw agricultural waste.
🏭 2. Pulp/Paper Mill: Processing the bagasse into fiber.
🥣 3. Pulp Extraction: Refining into pure natural pulp.
⚙️ 4. Factory: Thermoforming in our high-tech facility.
🍽️ 5. Product: Quality checking the final tableware.
♻️ 6. 100% Biodegradable: Ready to return to the earth.

The Science of Sugarcane Bagasse

Why our natural fiber is the ultimate packaging solution.

🌱 Agricultural Waste

Made from the fibrous residue left after sugarcane juice extraction. Zero tree cutting.

♻️ 90-Day Composting

Returns to the earth in 90-120 days in commercial composting facilities. Leaves no toxic residue.

🔥 Heat & Microwave Safe

Handles hot foods and liquids up to 100°C. Safe for microwave heating and freezer storage.

💧 Oil & Water Resistant

Naturally repels grease and moisture, maintaining structural integrity even with heavy curries.
